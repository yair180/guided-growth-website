# Guided Growth — Product context

> Quick-start derived from `~/Developer/yair-ai-files/Yair-Context/areas/guided-growth.md` and `YAIR.md`. Refine via `/impeccable teach` before the production redesign.

## Register

**Brand.** Marketing landing page at guidedgrowthos.com. Communicates, not transacts. Conversion goal = waitlist signup for the June 2026 MVP launch (50 founding users).

## What it is

Voice-first behavioral OS. Quick conversational check-ins with an AI coach that tracks your sleep, energy, habits, stress, routines, and surfaces patterns over time. Not a habit-tracker, not a journal app, not a wellness blog. Built deliberately against the addictive-app playbook: the goal is to get the user off their phone and into their life.

## Who it's for

Adults who want to improve their life but are tired of generic productivity apps and engagement-loop wellness software. People with goals that keep getting pushed aside, who want structure without rigidity, who know they're capable of more. Launch ICP is English-US. Founders, operators, busy professionals — the same people who burned out on Headspace, Day One, Notion templates, and habit trackers.

## Voice (three concrete words)

**Quiet. Embodied. Patient.**

- *Quiet:* no engagement traps, no notifications-by-default, no streak guilt. The voice on the page should not shout.
- *Embodied:* the product is voice-first because the founder is a real human coach who has talked to hundreds of clients. The page should feel like it has a body behind it.
- *Patient:* behavior change is long-arc. Nothing on the page should feel urgent, hype-y, or hustle-coded.

## Anti-references (NOT what we look like)

- **Headspace / Calm.** Soft gradients, illustrated clouds, mascot-ish. We're not consumer-meditation.
- **Notion / Linear / Vercel.** Pure-black monochrome, Inter, dev-tool aesthetic. We're not a tool for makers.
- **Generic SaaS landing page (current state).** White background, blue accent, gradient hero text, emoji feature cards, centered stack. Reads as Claude-designed because half the internet ships this.
- **Editorial-magazine.** Display serif + italic + drop caps + small mono labels + ruled separators. Saturated by 2026; Klim / Stripe / Notion adjacency.
- **YC orange / fintech navy + gold / crypto neon-on-black.** Category reflexes.

## Strategic principles

- Conversion lever is *credibility*, not novelty. The visitor needs to believe a real human stands behind the AI.
- Privacy / no-data-selling / no-ads is a real differentiator. Surface it without making it a section heading.
- "Hundreds of clients" — never specific small numbers in public copy.
- Multilingual voice (Hebrew / Spanish / English) is a demo capability, NOT a marketed differentiator. Launch ICP is English-US.
- Do not lead with founder bio on the marketing site. Lead with the product promise; the founder shows up as voice/tone.
