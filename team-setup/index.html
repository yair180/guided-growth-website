Hands-on · organize

Set up and organize your buckets

A bucket is one Claude-session topic. The steps inside it, top to bottom, are exactly what your Claude will work through.

Say: "Drag your most important step to the top. The board is the instructions, so whatever sits at the top is what your next session does first."

In your lane, right now

  • Find your buckets (each is a Claude-session topic)
  • Drag steps so the top step is what to do next
  • Edit a step's prompt or next-note if it's unclear
  • Click a status dot to set where each step stands

Hands-on · go live

Run your first step

Now let your Claude take the top step. Watch the board move while it works.

Say: "Tell your Claude to work the board. Watch the top step go in-progress, then done, live on the board. That's the loop you'll run every day."

The loop

  1. Tell Claude work my board (terminal) or read_board, work my top step (app)
  2. It reads your top step and starts it, marking it in-progress on the board
  3. It finishes and marks it done, then takes the new top step
  4. You reprioritize any time by dragging a step up
