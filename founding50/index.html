Guided Growth is everything I've learned about real change, built into one place. I made it for myself first. Now I want to put it in the hands of people who will genuinely benefit from it, and who can help shape its value and its vision.

Whether you've never tracked a habit or kept a journal in your life, or you've been doing it for years, this is built to meet you where you are. My intention is that you get real value from it either way.

Over time, this can replace the scattered apps and spreadsheets you use to track your life. Our roadmap covers ten connected areas of well-being, because you're one organism, not ten separate apps. We're not asking you to switch anything now. It's here to add to your life, and leaving your other tools, if and when it feels natural, is exactly what we'd hope for.

I'd love for it to give you something real, and to play a part, small or major, in your growth. In appreciation for your participation and feedback, your access is free for three months, and six months if it's still giving you value. And I want your honest take as you live with it, the good and the rough, so we can shape it together.

What I'm asking from you is small, and it's mostly just talking:

  • Onboarding is a five-minute chat by voice. Prefer not to talk or listen? Tap and type your way through instead, it all works the same.
  • Each morning, a one minute check-in: how you slept, your energy, your mood, and your stress.
  • Each night, a short conversation with your coach about your day and your habits, plus a little journaling. A couple of sentences, or my simple template: what am I proud of, what do I forgive myself for, and what am I grateful for.

Because it's voice-first, you don't even need to hold or look at your phone to have a free-flowing conversation. And it quietly turns into powerful insight over time.

Even though this is a mobile app, my intention is to help you use your phone way less.

All I ask is an honest two weeks. That's about how long it takes to feel the shift: a lot more awareness, and the real beginning of change in the direction you want to go. If it's not for you after that, no pressure and no hard feelings.

So let me be straight with you. If an honest two weeks doesn't genuinely pull at you, if you're not a little curious and excited to go for it, please don't take a spot. 50 is very few, and I want them to go to people who truly want to improve their life. Don't do this as a favor to me or to anyone. Do it because you can see it serving you. That's exactly the kind of person I'm hoping for.
